About me

I am a 25 year old, mostly autodidact, web-developer from Denmark with a passion for all things interactive. In in my spare time i jog, play/make games and read books about programming(At the moment i am studying Perl for system administration purposes).

Professionally i am a mixed front-end and PHP-backend developer with 5 years of experience in the field. I enjoy challenging work, and i often try out new libraries and experiment with the best solutions to continuously improve the projects i work on.

I am used to working in small teams collaborating with both designers and project managers to make sure the project gets done, in regards to this i have some Scrum experience.

My current coding bench of choice is Sublimetext with a bunch of addons, and homebrew snippets, on Mac OSX. Recently i have been thinking about going Vim/Linux as it seems fun tho. I switch it up for compiled languages as it makes more sense to use a IDE for that, like Eclipse or CodeBlocks, but i rarely have that need for anything but Actionscript at the moment.
