Hvid zone 2013

Developed to promote the educations for radioologi, bioanalytics and nursing, This is a realtime quiz app meant to work on both desktop and mobile browsers with a twist of Facebook.

The app leads the user through diagnosing and choosing a treatment of 40 Facebook friends in a given medical field. Based on how well the user does a score is given and if the score is high enough the user is recommended to apply for the given education.

Sag’s durch die Nuss 2011

This is not one of my prouder moments, as we hit a lot of walls during production, but the app ended up working and is definitely a proof that the web can be used for interesting things, hence why i am listing it here.

For the german firm Ültje we developed a Facebook application that allowed 1, or 2 people together on different computers, to record a video of them self as a nut, complete with image manipulation to put their face on the nut. The nut was controller via facetracking, and the mouth was controlled via the microphone(Southpark Canada style!). After recording either as a single person or as 2 people. the video got the recoded voices pitched via flash and the video was then added to a gallery.

Quite a mouthfull... anyway Ültje selected the funniest videos and rewarded the users with prices.
